Section 1.9 Term and Termination. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of 4 (four) years unless sooner terminated in accordance with this Section. Either party may terminate this Agreement: (a) upon 90 (ninety) days' prior written notice; (b) immediately, upon written notice, if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and such breach is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice; or (c) immediately, if the other party becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy protection.

The parties shall attempt to resolve any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement through good-faith negotiation for a period of not less than thirty (30) days. If the dispute cannot be resolved through negotiation, the parties agree to submit the dispute to binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association under its Commercial Arbitration Rules.
